# Limbic® Map, the 7 Types & the Motive→Code Cheat-Sheet

Source: Häusel, *Neuromarketing*. The operating core for choosing WHICH emotion to fire and HOW to dress it
in words, color, form, and imagery. Pick ONE dominant motive per asset and commit; hedging lands in the
"dead middle" and activates nothing.

## The Big 3 Emotional Systems

**BALANCE** (largest system; inhibiting, risk-limiting)
- Core motive: security, stability, belonging, constancy, order.
- Desires: protection, familiarity, the "herd", reliability, preservation.
- Fears: change, uncertainty, risk, loss, chaos, being alone.
- Neuro: Behavior Inhibition System; cortisol (rises with age); estrogen/oxytocin (stronger in women).
- Submodules: Care (Fürsorge), Bonding (Bindung).

**DOMINANCE** (activating, optimistic)
- Core motive: assertion, power, status, autonomy, achievement, victory.
- Desires: being better/bigger, control, recognition, ascent, efficiency, beating the competition.
- Fears: being controlled, dependence, oppression, status loss, powerlessness.
- Neuro: Behavior Activation System; testosterone (stronger in men, falls with age).
- Submodules: Hunt/Prey, Fighting.

**STIMULANCE** (activating, optimistic)
- Core motive: variety, curiosity, reward, discovery, fun.
- Desires: novelty, stimulation, individuality, spontaneity, experience, play.
- Fears: boredom, sameness, stagnation, uniformity.
- Neuro: mesolimbic reward system; dopamine (falls with age); novelty/sensation seeking.
- Submodule: Play.

**System dynamic:** Dominance + Stimulance push toward action and risk. Balance is the brake ("is this safe?
do I need this?"). Good copy fires the driver system AND disarms the Balance brake (guarantees, social proof,
trust seals). Especially mandatory on Dominance/Stimulance offers.

## The 3 Mixed Zones
- **ADVENTURE/THRILL** = Dominance + Stimulance: surpass yourself + discover new. Risk, courage, edge, conquest.
- **FANTASY/ENJOYMENT** = Stimulance + Balance: curiosity softened to dreaming, sensual pleasure, letting it come to you. Holistic, intuitive style.
- **DISCIPLINE/CONTROL** = Balance + Dominance: everything constant and predictable (Balance) + I set the rules (Dominance). Asceticism, order, precision, duty, thrift. Analytical, sequential style.

Axes: Balance(preserve) ⟷ Adventure(revolution); Dominance(egoistic) ⟷ Care/Bonding(altruistic); Stimulance(hedonistic now) ⟷ Discipline(ascetic).

## The 7 Limbic® Types (audience segmentation)
Personality = individual mix of the Big 3 (~50% innate, ~50% imprinted). Map distribution: Harmonizers ~2x more
common in women; Adventurer/Performer/Disciplined ~2x more common in men (overlapping curves, not absolutes).
The Hooka taxonomy maps 1:1:

| Type (Hooka label) | Driver | Core profile | Responds to |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Harmonizer (Balance/Care)** | Balance + Bonding | high social/family orientation, low status drive | safety, family, warmth, familiarity |
| **Open-Minded (Fantasy/Enjoyment)** | Balance + Stimulance | openness + sensual enjoyment | sensuality, gentle pleasure, quality, aesthetics |
| **Hedonist (Fun/Novelty)** | Stimulance | actively seeks new, individualist, spontaneous | novelty, individuality, trend, experience, cool |
| **Adventurer (Risk/Freedom)** | Stimulance + Dominance | high risk-tolerance, low impulse control | risk, freedom, edge, conquest |
| **Performer (Status/Dominance)** | Dominance | high achievement + status orientation, ambition | status, victory, efficiency, exclusivity, success |
| **Disciplined (Logic/Precision)** | Dominance + Balance | high duty, low consumption lust | reason, function, thrift, reliability, precision |
| **Traditionalist (Safety/Order)** | Balance | low future-orientation, wants order + security | tradition, the proven, security, home, constancy |

The buyer's emotional center decides, unconsciously, which brand messages even register as relevant. Same product
category, opposite Limbic profiles: e.g. Beck's beer (Adventure: three-master, defy the storm) vs. Radeberger
(Balance/Tradition: opera house, heritage exclusivity).

## MOTIVE → CODE CHEAT-SHEET (words, tone, color, form, imagery, web/CTA)

> Every code (word, color, form, image, sound) carries a culturally-learned implicit meaning that triggers its
> motive system. Keep all codes consistent on ONE motive = up to 10x neural multiplier.

### BALANCE / Harmonizer / Traditionalist
- **Words:** sicher, geborgen, vertraut, bewährt, zuverlässig, Heimat, Tradition, Familie, Schutz, gemeinsam, Vertrauen, seit [Jahr], Original, beständig, sorgenfrei, Garantie, natürlich, echt, Zuhause. (EN: safe, secure, trusted, proven, reliable, home, together, protected, since, original, guaranteed, genuine.)
- **Tone:** warm, soft, calm, caring, personal, reassuring.
- **Color:** warm muted tones; brown, soft green, gold/warm beige, muted blue (trust). Dark = calming.
- **Form:** round, soft, closed, organic, symmetric, flowing. No hard edges.
- **Imagery:** family, home, generations, nature, familiar everyday scenes, community/"herd", craft, history, warmth.
- **Web/CTA:** trust seals, guarantees, money-back, testimonials, "X happy customers", clear structure, no overwhelm, calming microcopy.

### DOMINANCE / Performer
- **Words:** Macht, Sieg, Erfolg, Leistung, Kontrolle, exklusiv, Elite, Nr. 1, dominieren, durchsetzen, Vorsprung, stärker, schneller, Profi, Premium, beherrschen, Effizienz, gewinnen, Performance. (EN: power, win, results, control, exclusive, elite, #1, dominate, edge, stronger, faster, pro, premium, master, efficiency.)
- **Tone:** confident, superior, direct, demanding, terse, uncompromising.
- **Color:** black, deep red, metallics (silver/anthracite), strong contrast, gold (status). Bright = activating.
- **Form:** angular, sharp, geometric, vertical/ascending, heavy, massive (heavy = valuable).
- **Imagery:** status symbols, summits/height, victory, technology, power poses, before/after ascent, beating rivals.
- **Web/CTA:** clear hierarchy, "join the top X", scarcity/exclusivity, performance numbers, comparison tables (winning), premium price as status signal.

### STIMULANCE / Hedonist
- **Words:** neu, entdecken, erleben, anders, einzigartig, Abwechslung, Spaß, kreativ, frisch, überraschend, Trend, spielen, individuell, jetzt, spontan, Inspiration. (EN: new, discover, experience, different, unique, variety, fun, creative, fresh, surprising, trend, play, individual, now.)
- **Tone:** playful, lively, cheeky, fast, surprising, unconventional, cool.
- **Color:** bright, saturated, high-contrast, unusual combos, glowing accents, gradients. Bright = activating.
- **Form:** dynamic, asymmetric, playful, unexpected, in-motion.
- **Imagery:** novelty/innovation, movement, variety, experience, lifestyle, creativity, pattern interrupts, color.
- **Web/CTA:** emphasize novelty ("brand new", "be first"), gamification, variability, surprise, animation/interaction, limited editions.

### ADVENTURE/THRILL (Dom+Stim) / Adventurer
- **Words:** Risiko, Grenze, Freiheit, Eroberung, Mut, wild, ungezähmt, Herausforderung, durchstarten, Adrenalin.
- **Color/Form:** strong red/orange/black, dynamic sharp forms. **Imagery:** extremes, nature-as-adversary, solo hero, storms/mountains/sea, speed.

### FANTASY/ENJOYMENT (Stim+Bal) / Open-Minded
- **Words:** genießen, sinnlich, träumen, verwöhnen, sanft, Auszeit, Hingabe, zart, Moment, Leichtigkeit.
- **Color/Form:** soft warm tones, pastel, flowing forms. **Imagery:** sensuality, enjoyment rituals, soft textures, relaxation, aesthetics, daydreams.

### DISCIPLINE/CONTROL (Bal+Dom) / Disciplined
- **Words:** Vernunft, Funktion, präzise, effizient, durchdacht, logisch, sparsam, sinnvoll, Kontrolle, geprüft, rational, verlässlich.
- **Tone:** factual, sober, clear, evidence-based. **Color:** blue (cool, factual), grey, anthracite, reduced palettes, lots of whitespace. **Form:** clean, straight, geometric, minimal, structured. **Imagery:** function, technology, order, clarity.

**Color anchors (LTC-learned):** Blue = cool/factual/trust. Bright = activating. Dark = calming. Red = attention/energy (culture-dependent). Watch international meanings (red = luck in China; blue = "evil" in China; white = mourning in Asia).

## Limbic Personae (target-figure briefs)
A Persona is a prototypical, "graspable" figure built from validated audience data, centered on the **motive level**
(drivers & barriers) and personality, not demographics. Build one before a campaign: name, Limbic Type, core motives,
fears, desired after-state, the words/colors/images that resonate, and the objections their Balance brake raises.
Use it as the single "creative target" every asset is written to. (Concept: Nymphenburg Limbic Personae.)
